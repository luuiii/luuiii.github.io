> Jia Liu is a composer and computer music performer based in Karlsruhe, Germany. She composes for acoustic instruments as well as electroacoustic music and other media. Since 2019, she has been programming and performing live computer music. Currently, she is focusing on algorithmic composition, music with autonomous systems and communicative algorithms.

She studied composition and music informatics at the Conservatory of Music in Shanghai and at the University of Music Karlsruhe, at the Institute for Music Informatics and Musicology (IMWI).
